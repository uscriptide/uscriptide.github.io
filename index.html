What is USC Riptide Summit & what should I expect?

USC Riptide Summit is a joint project between the entrepreneurial and design-focused student organizations at the University of Southern California. Powered by Adobe & USC Iovine and Young Academy with USC Design for America, USC AIGA, Spark SC and LavaLab, our goal is to inspire students to explore the intersections of technology and design through an immersive day-long summit. Speakers and panelists will discuss the processes, tools, and techniques used to build and empower multidisciplinary teams and share their experiences with working in the industry.

Who can attend USC Riptide summit?

USC Riptide Summit is open to college students within the Los Angeles areas, including USC, UCLA, ArtCenter College of Design, etc.
